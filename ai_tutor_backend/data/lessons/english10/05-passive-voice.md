# Passive Voice - Câu bị động

## 1. Khái niệm

### 1.1. Câu chủ động (Active Voice)
Chủ ngữ thực hiện hành động

**S + V + O**
- John **writes** a letter.

### 1.2. Câu bị động (Passive Voice)
Chủ ngữ chịu tác động của hành động

**S + be + V3/ed (+ by O)**
- A letter **is written** by John.

### 1.3. Khi nào dùng câu bị động?
- Không biết hoặc không quan trọng ai thực hiện hành động
- Muốn nhấn mạnh đối tượng chịu tác động
- Văn phong trang trọng, khoa học

## 2. Cấu trúc câu bị động theo thì

### 2.1. Present Simple
**Active:** S + V(s/es) + O
**Passive:** S + am/is/are + V3/ed

**Ví dụ:**
- Active: They **clean** the room every day.
- Passive: The room **is cleaned** every day.

### 2.2. Present Continuous
**Active:** S + am/is/are + V-ing + O
**Passive:** S + am/is/are + being + V3/ed

**Ví dụ:**
- Active: She **is writing** a letter.
- Passive: A letter **is being written** by her.

### 2.3. Present Perfect
**Active:** S + have/has + V3/ed + O
**Passive:** S + have/has + been + V3/ed

**Ví dụ:**
- Active: They **have finished** the project.
- Passive: The project **has been finished**.

### 2.4. Past Simple
**Active:** S + V2/ed + O
**Passive:** S + was/were + V3/ed

**Ví dụ:**
- Active: He **wrote** the book in 2010.
- Passive: The book **was written** in 2010.

### 2.5. Past Continuous
**Active:** S + was/were + V-ing + O
**Passive:** S + was/were + being + V3/ed

**Ví dụ:**
- Active: They **were building** the bridge.
- Passive: The bridge **was being built**.

### 2.6. Past Perfect
**Active:** S + had + V3/ed + O
**Passive:** S + had + been + V3/ed

**Ví dụ:**
- Active: She **had completed** the task.
- Passive: The task **had been completed**.

### 2.7. Future Simple (will)
**Active:** S + will + V + O
**Passive:** S + will + be + V3/ed

**Ví dụ:**
- Active: They **will announce** the results tomorrow.
- Passive: The results **will be announced** tomorrow.

### 2.8. Be going to
**Active:** S + am/is/are + going to + V + O
**Passive:** S + am/is/are + going to + be + V3/ed

**Ví dụ:**
- Active: He **is going to repair** the car.
- Passive: The car **is going to be repaired**.

### 2.9. Modal Verbs (can, could, may, might, must, should)
**Active:** S + modal + V + O
**Passive:** S + modal + be + V3/ed

**Ví dụ:**
- Active: You **must do** this work.
- Passive: This work **must be done**.

- Active: She **can solve** the problem.
- Passive: The problem **can be solved**.

## 3. Bảng tổng hợp công thức

| Thì/Cấu trúc | Active | Passive |
|--------------|--------|---------|
| Present Simple | S + V(s/es) | S + am/is/are + V3/ed |
| Present Continuous | S + am/is/are + V-ing | S + am/is/are + being + V3/ed |
| Present Perfect | S + have/has + V3/ed | S + have/has + been + V3/ed |
| Past Simple | S + V2/ed | S + was/were + V3/ed |
| Past Continuous | S + was/were + V-ing | S + was/were + being + V3/ed |
| Past Perfect | S + had + V3/ed | S + had + been + V3/ed |
| Future Simple | S + will + V | S + will + be + V3/ed |
| Modal Verbs | S + modal + V | S + modal + be + V3/ed |

## 4. Các trường hợp đặc biệt

### 4.1. Câu có hai tân ngữ
**Active:** S + V + O1 (person) + O2 (thing)

Có 2 cách chuyển bị động:

**Cách 1:** O1 + be + V3/ed + O2
**Cách 2:** O2 + be + V3/ed + to/for + O1

**Ví dụ:**
- Active: She gave me a book.
- Passive 1: I was given a book (by her).
- Passive 2: A book was given to me (by her).

### 4.2. Động từ tri giác: see, watch, hear, notice
**Active:** S + V + O + V(infinitive)/V-ing
**Passive:** S + be + V3/ed + to V/V-ing

**Ví dụ:**
- Active: I saw him **cross** the road.
- Passive: He was seen **to cross** the road.

- Active: I heard her **singing**.
- Passive: She was heard **singing**.

### 4.3. Động từ sai khiến: make, let, help
**Active:** S + make/let + O + V(infinitive)
**Passive:** S + be + made/let + to V

**Ví dụ:**
- Active: They made him work hard.
- Passive: He was made to work hard.

- Active: She let me go.
- Passive: I was let to go.

### 4.4. Câu mệnh lệnh
**Active:** V + O
**Passive:** Let + O + be + V3/ed

**Ví dụ:**
- Active: Open the door.
- Passive: Let the door be opened.

### 4.5. Câu bị động với "by"
- Không cần "by" khi người thực hiện không quan trọng hoặc không xác định
  - English is spoken all over the world.
  - The car was stolen yesterday.

- Cần "by" khi người thực hiện quan trọng
  - "Romeo and Juliet" was written by Shakespeare.
  - This bridge was designed by a famous architect.

## 5. Passive Voice với các động từ đặc biệt

### 5.1. Say, believe, think, know, report...
**Cách 1:** It + be + V3/ed + that + clause
**Cách 2:** S + be + V3/ed + to V/to have V3

**Ví dụ:**
- Active: People say that he is rich.
- Passive 1: It is said that he is rich.
- Passive 2: He is said to be rich.

- Active: They believed that she had left.
- Passive 1: It was believed that she had left.
- Passive 2: She was believed to have left.

## 6. Lưu ý quan trọng

1. **Không dùng bị động với:**
   - Động từ nội động (không có tân ngữ): go, come, sleep, happen, occur
   - Động từ chỉ trạng thái: have (có), seem, appear

2. **Động từ bất quy tắc:**
   Cần nhớ V3 của động từ bất quy tắc
   - write → written
   - break → broken
   - take → taken

3. **By + agent:**
   - by + người/vật thực hiện hành động
   - with + công cụ: The door was opened with a key.
